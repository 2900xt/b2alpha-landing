## Why "DNS + Hosted Conversations" Wins

### The DNS Analogy

**DNS solved discovery for the web:**
- Type a name → get an IP address
- Universal, open, essential infrastructure
- Free to use, critical for everything

**B2Alpha Registry solves discovery for agents:**
- Query a need → get business endpoint
- Universal, open, essential infrastructure
- Free/cheap to use, critical for agent commerce

### The Stripe Analogy

**Stripe made payments easy:**
- Developers integrate once
- Stripe handles complexity (fraud, compliance, global payments)
- Takes a cut of transactions

**B2Alpha Conversation Host makes agent commerce easy:**
- Agents integrate once
- We handle complexity (history, escrow, disputes)
- Takes a cut of hosted transactions

### Why Both Together?

1. **Registry gets distribution:** Every agent needs discovery. Free/cheap lookup means everyone uses us.
2. **Conversations capture value:** Complex transactions need hosting. That's where money flows.
3. **Network effects:** More businesses in registry → more agents use us → more businesses sign up.
4. **Lock-in through value:** Escrow, history, and reputation data make switching costly.

| Service | Price | Margin |
|---------|-------|--------|

| Business subscription | $99-999/mo | 90%+ |
| Escrow fee | 2.9% + $0.30 | ~50% (after Stripe) |
| Dispute resolution | $25-100 | 60% |

**Unit Economics Example:**

Contractor quote conversation:
- 15 messages × $0.03 = $0.45
- Escrow on $20,000 job = $580 + $0.30 = $580.30
- **Total revenue: $580.75**
- **Margin after Stripe: ~$290**

### Why This Works

1. **Free registry removes friction:** No barrier to getting businesses and agents on platform
2. **Value capture on complex deals:** Where the real money is
3. **Escrow is pure margin:** Money sitting in our account, earning float
4. **Network effects compound:** Each business makes the registry more valuable

## Risk Analysis

### Risk 1: Big Tech Enters
**Threat:** Google, Apple, or Microsoft build agent commerce layer
**Mitigation:** 
- Move fast, establish network effects before they notice
- Open protocol makes us the Switzerland (neutral)
- Big tech often can't be neutral (conflicts with their businesses)

### Risk 2: AI Model Providers Build It
**Threat:** OpenAI or Anthropic add business registry to their agents
**Mitigation:**
- They want to be model providers, not commerce infrastructure
- Neutral third party is more trustworthy than model provider
- We can integrate with all models, they're locked to their own

### Risk 3: Businesses Don't Adopt
**Threat:** Businesses don't see value in listing
**Mitigation:**
- Free registry tier removes friction
- Start with motivated verticals (contractors, professionals)
- Show clear ROI through hosted conversations

### Risk 4: Agents Don't Need Us
**Threat:** Agents figure out discovery without registry
**Mitigation:**
- Web scraping is unreliable and legally risky
- Structured capability data is better than guessing
- Escrow requires trusted third party regardless
